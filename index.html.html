How It Works

8-step AI pipeline that delivers perfect dubbing with emotion, tone, and lip-sync accuracy.

01
🎙️
Speech to Text
AI extracts and transcribes speech from your video with high accuracy.
02
🌐
Text Translation
Translates the transcribed text into your selected target language.
03
🔊
Text to Speech
Converts translated text into natural-sounding speech using AI voices.
04
😤
Emotion & Tone Detection
Detects emotion, mood, and seriousness to match voice tone perfectly.
05
👤
Age & Gender Matching
Identifies character age and gender for accurate voice assignment.
06
👄
Lip-Sync Adjustment
Synchronizes dubbed audio with lip movements frame by frame.
07
🎵
Audio Balancing
Balances background music, sound effects, and dubbed voice perfectly.
08
📝
Subtitle Generation
Generates synchronized subtitles in Urdu, English, and more languages.
